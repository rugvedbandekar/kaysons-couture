Our Story

Dressed with purpose, worn with pride.

Kaysons Couture has been the trusted destination for men who understand the quiet power of a well-fitted garment. We carry a carefully curated range of readymade menswear — from formal suits and ethnic sherwanis to everyday kurtas and casual wear.

Every piece in our store is hand-selected for fabric quality, cut precision, and lasting style. Because we believe the right attire is not just what you wear — it is how you carry yourself into every room.

Kaysons Couture is owned and operated by Neema Rajendra Narvekar.
